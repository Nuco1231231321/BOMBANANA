## Your First BOMBANANA! Session

So you've downloaded the Demo, rounded up two friends, and you're staring at the main menu. What now? This guide walks you through every step of your first session — from launch to your first successful (or explosive) defusal.

![BOMBANANA! First Session](/images/screenshots/ss_858b009c283bfaac07f526309cb8a68c0c284278.thumb.jpg)

## Before You Launch

### System Check (All 3 Players)

- [ ] **Steam installed and logged in** — All three players need Steam accounts
- [ ] **Demo downloaded** — Get it from [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4747510/BOMBANANA_Demo/) (free, ~300 MB)
- [ ] **Microphone working** — Test in Steam → Settings → Voice → Test Microphone
- [ ] **Headphones on** — Prevents audio echo and feedback
- [ ] **Steam Friends online** — All three must be friends on Steam and visible

### Role Assignment

Decide who plays which role BEFORE launching:

| If You Are... | Try This Role | Why |
|---------------|---------------|-----|
| Good at giving clear instructions | **Deaf Monkey** | You're the communication bridge |
| Good at puzzles and manuals | **Mute Monkey** | You hold all the answers |
| Steady hands, calm under pressure | **Blind Monkey** | You physically defuse the bomb |
| New to gaming entirely | **Blind Monkey** | Simplest mental load per action |

> **Pro tip:** Don't lock into roles forever. Rotate after 3-4 bombs so everyone understands each role's challenges. This builds empathy and improves team performance.

## Launch Sequence

1. **Host player** launches BOMBANANA! Demo from Steam Library
2. **Host** selects "Play" → "Host Game" → creates a lobby
3. **Host** opens Steam overlay (Shift+Tab) → Friends list → right-click each friend → "Invite to Game"
4. **Friends** accept the invite; the game launches for them
5. All three players appear in the lobby — each selects a different role (Blind / Deaf / Mute)
6. **Host** clicks "Start" — the van doors close, and the first bomb appears

## The First 60 Seconds

When the bomb loads, follow this exact sequence:

### 0-10 sec: Blind Sweeps
The Blind Monkey runs their hands across the bomb surface. The screen shows only grainy outlines. Report out loud:
- *"I count... 4 modules"*
- *"Feeling a wire panel on the left, a button grid in the center, and two more on the right"*

### 10-20 sec: Deaf Identifies
The Deaf visually confirms each module type and calls them out:
- *"Module 1: Wire panel — 5 wires"*
- *"Module 2: Button grid — 3×2"*
- *"Module 3: Keypad — 4×3"*
- *"Module 4: Switch array — 4 switches"*

### 20-30 sec: Mute Locates Solutions
While the Blind and Deaf map the bomb, the Mute opens the manual and finds the correct sections for each module type. Bookmark or note the page numbers mentally.

### 30-60 sec: Start Defusing
Pick the hardest-looking module first — you have the most time and the fewest strikes right now. The communication chain begins:
- **Mute** → gestures the solution
- **Deaf** → interprets and speaks instruction
- **Blind** → repeats, confirms, executes

## Your First Defusal Attempt: What to Expect

Most teams fail their first 2-3 bombs. That's normal. Here's what typically goes wrong and how to handle it:

| Common First-Bomb Scenario | How to Handle |
|---------------------------|---------------|
| Blind cuts the wrong wire | Count wires out loud, touching each one. Deaf must confirm visually before saying "cut" |
| Mute's gesture is misunderstood | Slow down. One gesture at a time. Wait for Deaf's thumbs-up before next gesture |
| Deaf calls wrong instruction | Mute notices the Blind doing the wrong thing → Mute does thumbs-down vigorously → Deaf stops and re-reads the gesture |
| Panic when timer hits 30 sec | Deaf announces: "30 seconds — 2 modules left. Focus. No confirmations needed — just follow my voice" |

## After the Bomb

Whether you defused it or exploded:

1. **Celebrate** (or laugh at) the result
2. **Debrief** for exactly 2 minutes:
   - Which module was hardest?
   - Where did communication break?
   - What gesture was unclear?
3. **Rotate roles** (optional — recommended after 3 bombs)
4. **Start the next bomb**
