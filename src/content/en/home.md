## What is BOMBANANA!?

BOMBANANA! is a **3-player asymmetric co-op bomb defusal game** developed by **Lefto Studio**. You and two friends take on the roles of three monkeys, each with a specific sensory disability, working together to defuse a banana-shaped bomb before time runs out.

![BOMBANANA! Game Banner](https://shared.fastly.steamstatic.com/store_item_assets/steam/apps/4747510/header.jpg)

## The Three Roles

| Role | Disability | Ability | Primary Task |
|------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| 🙈 **Blind Monkey** | Cannot see colors or read text | Can hear and speak | Physically interacts with the bomb |
| 🙉 **Deaf Monkey** | Cannot hear anything | Can see and speak | Relays instructions between Mute and Blind |
| 🙊 **Mute Monkey** | Cannot speak | Can see and hear, holds the manual | Gestures the solution to the Deaf |

## Communication Chain

The information flow in BOMBANANA! follows this chain:

1. **Mute Monkey** reads the defusal manual
2. **Mute Monkey** gestures the solution to the Deaf Monkey
3. **Deaf Monkey** interprets gestures and verbally instructs the Blind Monkey
4. **Blind Monkey** executes the defusal on the bomb

## Why This Game Is Special

- **Asymmetric co-op** — each player has a completely different experience
- **In-game voice chat restrictions** — game mechanics enforce the role limitations
- **Custom communication systems** — teams must invent their own sign language
- **Free Mode** — design your own bomb puzzles for infinite replayability
