## Bomb Modules in BOMBANANA!

Each bomb in BOMBANANA! contains multiple **modules** — independent puzzles that must be solved to defuse the bomb. Every module has:

- **Unique rules** — different logic, different solution
- **Panic effects** — what happens when you make a mistake
- **Role-specific interactions** — who does what for this module

## Module Categories

### Logic Puzzles
Modules that require deductive reasoning. The Mute Monkey reads the manual's logic tree, gestures the path, and the Blind Monkey executes.

### Sequence Modules
Follow a specific order of actions. Missing a step or doing them out of order triggers a strike.

### Memory Games
Remember previous states and compare them. These get harder as more information accumulates.

### Timed Challenges
Complete the module within a time window. The Deaf Monkey's timer monitoring is critical here.

### Communication Tests
Designed specifically to exploit the communication gaps between roles. These are the hardest modules.

## Module Difficulty Progression

| Level | Modules per Bomb | New Mechanics |
|-------|-----------------|---------------|
| 1-3 | 3-4 | Basic logic, single-step solutions |
| 4-6 | 4-5 | Multi-step sequences, memory elements |
| 7-9 | 5-6 | Timed challenges, panic effect chains |
| 10+ | 6-8 | Communication tests, combined mechanics |

## General Module Strategy

1. **Identify the module type** — Deaf Monkey calls out what they see
2. **Locate in manual** — Mute Monkey finds the correct section
3. **Plan the communication** — determine what gestures are needed
4. **Execute step by step** — Blind Monkey follows instructions
5. **Confirm completion** — verify the module light turns green

> **Pro tip:** In Free Mode, practice individual modules one at a time before combining them in full bomb scenarios.
