## The Blind Monkey 🙈

**Role:** *"See No Evil"*

The Blind Monkey is the **only character who can physically touch and interact with the bomb**. However, the bomb appears as a blurry, grayscale outline — you cannot see colors, read any text on screens, or distinguish between different wire types by sight alone.

### What You Can Do

- **Touch the bomb** — physically interact with modules, wires, buttons
- **Hear** your teammates' voice communications
- **Speak** to ask questions and confirm instructions

### What You Cannot Do

- **See colors** — every wire looks gray, every light looks the same brightness
- **Read text** — any on-screen labels, numbers, or instructions are invisible to you
- **See module details** — you can only feel the physical shape and layout

## Core Strategy

### 1. Trust Your Deaf Teammate Completely

You are entirely dependent on the Deaf Monkey's verbal instructions. If you don't trust their callouts, the bomb will explode. Build a rhythm of:

- **Listen** to the instruction
- **Repeat** it back for confirmation
- **Execute** only after confirmation

### 2. Learn the Physical Layout

Each bomb has a consistent physical layout. Spend your first few seconds feeling around:

- How many modules are there?
- Where are they positioned?
- What shapes can you distinguish?

### 3. Ask Specific Questions

Don't ask "What do I do?" — ask "Which wire should I cut, the top or bottom one?" Specific questions get specific answers.

### 4. Use Spatial Language

Establish a shared vocabulary for positions:
- "Top left module"
- "The wire closest to me"
- "Clockwise from the red button"

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Acting before confirming | Always repeat the instruction back first |
| Panicking under time pressure | Slow is smooth, smooth is fast |
| Ignoring module boundaries | Feel the edges of each module carefully |
| Not communicating confusion | If you don't understand, say so immediately |

## Advanced Tips

- **Develop tactile mnemonics** — associate physical shapes with names
- **Count out loud** — when following multi-step sequences, vocalize each step
- **Trust the process** — the Mute Monkey has the manual, they know the answer
