## The Deaf Monkey 🙉

**Role:** *"Hear No Evil"*

The Deaf Monkey is the **critical communication bridge** between the Mute and Blind monkeys. You can see the bomb clearly and speak instructions, but you cannot hear anything — not your teammates, not the game audio, not the ticking timer.

### What You Can Do

- **See everything** — the bomb, all modules, colors, text, and lights are clearly visible
- **Speak** — give verbal instructions to the Blind Monkey
- **Observe** the Mute Monkey's gestures

### What You Cannot Do

- **Hear anything** — no voice chat, no game sounds, no audio cues of any kind

## Core Strategy

### 1. Master Gesture Reading

The Mute Monkey will use gestures to convey information. You must:

- **Watch constantly** — don't look away from the Mute
- **Learn the gesture system** your team establishes
- **Confirm interpretations** by nodding or using pre-agreed signals

### 2. Translate Accurately

Your job is to convert the Mute's gestures into clear, actionable instructions for the Blind Monkey:

- Use **short, precise commands**
- Break complex sequences into **step-by-step instructions**
- Verify the Blind Monkey **repeats back** before executing

### 3. Monitor the Bomb

You're the only one who can see the full state of the bomb:

- Watch for **timer changes**
- Notice **module status indicators**
- Spot **panic effects** as they trigger

## Communication Flow

```
Mute Monkey (gestures) → YOU (interpret) → Blind Monkey (execute)
                                                    ↓
                                              YOU (confirm result)
                                                    ↓
                                              Mute Monkey (next step)
```

## The Gesture System

Work with your Mute partner to establish gestures for:

| Information | Example Gesture |
|-------------|----------------|
| Wire color | Point to clothing color, or use color-coded cards |
| Number | Hold up fingers |
| Direction (left/right/up/down) | Pointing |
| Module type | Unique hand shape per module type |
| Confirmation | Thumbs up / thumbs down |
| Urgency | Rapid waving |
| "I don't understand" | Shrug gesture |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Looking at the bomb instead of the Mute | Face the Mute, glance at bomb only when needed |
| Guessing the Mute's meaning | Ask them to repeat the gesture until you're sure |
| Over-explaining to the Blind | Keep instructions short and actionable |
| Missing panic effect triggers | Scan the bomb status every few seconds |
