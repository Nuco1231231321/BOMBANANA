## Update History

### Demo Launch — June 15, 2026

**Version:** Demo v1.0

The BOMBANANA! Demo launched on Steam as part of the June 2026 Steam Next Fest.

**What's Included:**
- Full 3-player online co-op gameplay
- All three asymmetric roles: Blind, Deaf, and Mute Monkey
- A selection of campaign levels (beginner to intermediate)
- Core bomb module types
- In-game voice chat with role-based restrictions
- Free Mode for designing custom bombs
- English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and 10+ other languages

**Known Demo Limitations:**
- Limited campaign levels (full campaign coming with the release)
- Partial module selection (more modules in the full game)
- Free Mode limited to demo-available modules
